  • Arrival

  • How early should I arrive for a class?

    Please arrive early to the studio to get settled but not TOO early. Arriving 10 mins early is plenty.

  • Where can I wait once I arrive?

    We do NOT have a waiting room + classes are often timed only 15 to 30 minutes apart.

  • Will I be allowed in if I am late for a class?

    You will not be admitted if you arrive after the 8 minute warm up.

  • Classes

  • Can I bring my phone to the class?

    You can, but phones are not allowed in the main room.

  • How I keep my sneakers on?

    Our classes are practiced without shoes. Socks are optional

  • Is there a shower I can use after class?

    We do not have showers. Sweaty is sexy.

  • I don't own a mat, gloves, or hand wraps. What should I do?

    Mats + gloves provided. Speed wraps are mandatory and available for purchase: $5. We do not wrap hands.

  • Payments

  • How should I pay for the classes?

    We only accept credit cards. NO cash.

  • My classes are expiring soon. Can I extend the date?

    We do NOT extend class packages. The expiration date is FIRM.

  • Cancelation

  • How long do I have to cancel a class?

    You MUST cancel your class at least 12 hours before you are scheduled. If you do not, you WILL lose the class. (see below for details)

Our Policies at Box + Flow

Return/Refund Policy
We do not offer refunds on purchases.
Transfers
All classes purchased are not available to transfer to other clients
Lost + Found
Box + Flow is not responsible for lost items. but we do keep a lost+found. call or email if something is left
Late Policy
Classes start on time + you will not be admitted after the 8 minute warmup
No Show + Late Cancel Policy
If you are more than 8 minutes late, your spot will be given away to someone on wait list. you MUST cancel your class 12 HOURS before. if a cancellation is made after this time, the class will be removed from your account. or if you are on an "unlimited-pack"-- a 15$ fee will be charged to your account
